Aarti

Aarti is a Hindu religious ritual of worship, a part of puja, in which light from wicks soaked in ghee or camphor is offered to one or more deities. Aartis also refer to the songs sung in praise of the deity, when lamps are being offered.

Eucharist

The eucharist is when Christians eat bread and drink wine in remembrance of Jesus.

Typically held on Sundays, found throughout Christian world, although interpretations differ.

Prayer

Prayer is found in multiple religious settings, and generally involves communication with a divinity.

Prayers can be private or public, formal or informal.

Church

Churches are Christian buildings for meeting in worship.

Churches take many shapes and sizes. They have often been purpose built, but smaller groups may adapt existing buildings.
